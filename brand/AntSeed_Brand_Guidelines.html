01 · Origin Story

Myrmecochory

The brand draws from a process in nature called myrmecochory — seed dispersal by ants. Plants produce seeds with nutrient-rich coatings called elaiosomes. Ants carry these seeds to their nests, eat the coating, and discard the seed in fertile ground. The seed grows. Everyone profits. No one controls.

In nature, ants don't own the seeds they carry. They don't control where the plant grows. They simply move value to where it's needed — and the whole ecosystem thrives.

AntSeed works the same way. Providers seed AI services onto an open network. Consumers discover and consume them. The protocol routes value. No company sits in the middle extracting rent.

The Seed

An AI service — inference, a skilled agent, a workflow. Anything a provider offers to the network.

The Ant

The protocol. It discovers, routes, and settles. It carries value without owning it.

The Colony

The network of peers. No queen. No hierarchy. Collective intelligence through individual action.

05 · Voice & Tone

AntSeed speaks like a builder — technical but not academic, confident but not arrogant. We explain complex things simply and let the work speak.

Builder, Not Marketer

We show code, not pitch decks. We ship, then talk about it. Technical credibility comes first.

Clear, Not Clever

"Peer-to-peer AI inference" not "democratizing the future of intelligence." Say what it does.

Confident, Not Loud

We state facts. We don't hype. "The protocol routes 10K requests/sec" not "BLAZING FAST!!!"

Inclusive, Not Exclusive

"Anyone can seed" — we build for the widest possible participation. No gatekeeping.

Open Source Native

GitHub over Medium. README over whitepaper. Commits over announcements. Show your work.

P2P Philosophy

"Everyone profits. No one controls." This is the north star. Every piece of writing should feel like it came from the colony, not a CEO.

Do write
  • "AntSeed is a P2P protocol for AI services"
  • "Plug in any LLM on one side, any agent on the other"
  • "Providers compete on price and reputation"
  • "Everyone profits. No one controls."
  • "Seed your services. Consume from the colony."
Don't write
  • "Revolutionizing AI with blockchain synergy"
  • "The Uber of AI inference"
  • "Disrupting Big Tech's monopoly"
  • "Web3-powered next-gen AI platform"
  • "Join our community of innovators!"

06 · Usage Rules

Do
  • Use on dark backgrounds (#0a0e14 preferred)
  • Maintain clear space equal to the ant mark height
  • Use the mint ant mark on dark backgrounds
  • Use the dark ant mark on light backgrounds
  • Keep the wordmark always in uppercase
  • Use "ANT" in white/dark, "SEED" in mint/dark
Don't
  • Place on busy or photographic backgrounds
  • Rotate, skew, or distort the mark
  • Change the color split in the wordmark
  • Add effects like drop shadows or gradients
  • Use the mint mark on light backgrounds
  • Write "Antseed", "antSeed", or "ANTSEED" as one color

Naming Conventions

Brand name: AntSeed (camelCase, always capitalized A and S)
In code/CLI: antseed (all lowercase)
Twitter/social: @AntSeedAI
Domain: antseed.com
GitHub org: github.com/AntSeed
Token: ANT (all caps)
Protocol ref: "the AntSeed protocol" or "the AntSeed network"
Tagline: "A Peer-to-Peer AI Services Network"
Motto: "Everyone profits. No one controls."
